# Welcome to Taqeem (تقييم) Wiki

**Taqeem** is an industrial-grade evaluation system designed for modern schools and educators. Whether you are hosting a small classroom contest or a large-scale school-wide competition, Taqeem provides the precision and impact needed to celebrate excellence.

## 🌟 Key Benefits for Your School

- **Efficiency**: Automate the tedious parts of competition management—from roster imports to score calculations.
- **Multilingual Excellence**: Full support for both Arabic and English, ensuring inclusivity for all participants and judges.
- **Real-time Insights**: Data syncs instantly across all devices. Admins can watch progress live as judges submit their evaluations.
- **Multi-Tenant Architecture**: Manage multiple competitions (Robotics, Spelling Bee, Sports, etc.) from a single unified platform.
- **Private & Secure**: Your school owns its data. Taqeem runs on your private cloud infrastructure, ensuring student privacy is always protected.
- **Device Agnostic**: No app store required. It works seamlessly on laptops, tablets, and smartphones as a Progressive Web App (PWA).
