# Judge Guide

Welcome, Judge! Your role is critical to the success of this competition. Taqeem is designed to make your evaluation process smooth and focused.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: Use the credentials provided by your school administrator.
- **Competition Selection**: Once logged in, you will be automatically placed into your assigned competition.

## 2. Navigating the Roster
- The main screen shows the **Student Roster**.
- You can scroll through the list or use the **Search Bar** to find a student by name or number.
- Students you have already evaluated may be highlighted or moved in the list depending on the school's configuration.

## 3. Performing an Evaluation
- Click on a student's name to open the **Evaluation Panel**.
- **Grading**: Use the sliders or input fields to assign points for each criterion.
- **Notes**: You can add comments to provide feedback for the student or for admin review.
- **Save/Submit**: Once finished, click the "Submit" or "Save" button. Your score is immediately sent to the central database.

## 4. Tips for Success
- **Device**: Taqeem works best on tablets or iPads. If you're on a phone, use it in landscape mode for a better view.
- **Real-time**: If you make a mistake, you can usually reopen the evaluation for that student and update the score, provided the admin has not locked the competition.
- **Language**: Use the toggle in the top-right corner to switch between Arabic and English if needed.
